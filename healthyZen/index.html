HealthyZen

Promote and Sustain a Healthier and Happier, Zen-like, You

HealthyZen integrates with the Withings scale and Apple Health App to provide you with data that promotes a healthier and happier you.

  • HealthyZen connects with your Withing scale weight data to automaticaly graph your weight.
  • It connects with the Apple Health App to graph the number of steps you take.
  • When you enter your mood, HealthyZen will graph that, too.
  • See all of these graphs in one place, and discover correlations in your health and mood, to promote and sustain a healthier and happier you.
